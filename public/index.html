How we ship

Three rules. Worth repeating.

01

One tool, one job

No suites pretending to be platforms. Each app is small enough that one person can hold the whole thing in their head — including the customer.

02

Customer owns the data

Plain CSV exports from every screen. No data hostage at end-of-trial. If you leave, you leave with your records.

03

Priced like tools, not SaaS

Flat monthly. Unlimited users. No tiered features held back to push you up the ladder. If we add it, you get it.
